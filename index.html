03 · How It Works

Geometry,
not guessing.

ARBITER measures semantic coherence — geometric relationships in 72-dimensional space. No training on outcomes. No probability. No hallucination surface.

01 · Query
Describe the condition
Not just the diagnosis. The mechanisms, history, symptoms, what’s been tried. The more precise the query, the more precise the geometry.
02 · Candidates
Name what you want ranked
Treatments, supplements, protocols, hypotheses. The engine doesn’t know which are “right” — it measures geometric fit to your condition description.
03 · Score
The geometry returns a float
Between 0 and 1. Not a recommendation. Not medical advice. A coherence score you bring to your care team as a starting point for conversation.
